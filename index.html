Tathagata Karmakar

I am a graduate student in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Rochester and an affiliated student researcher at Chapman University. I work with Prof. Andrew Jordan. My research interests lie in physics informed machine learning, superresolution imaging, quantum measurements, and continuously monitored systems. In my spare time, I like to make digital illustrations. To get a glimpse of my research work, please look at the projects.
